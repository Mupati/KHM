What Is The Aim Of This Ministry

Kingdom Harvesters Ministries is an end time charismatic and a “Touch Lives” inclined church. The aim of the church is to bring back to the body of Christ the urgency of the end times, righteous living, love, care and strong fellowship among brethren, connecting them to their God-given destinies and dreams as it is in heaven through the gospel of the Kingdom, ultimately bringing glory to God..
