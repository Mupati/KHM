Vision

Touching lives everywhere with the Gospel of the Kingdom of God (Body, Soul, Spirit), to win souls to Christ and disciple renewed souls to do the work of the ministry (Ephesians. 4:12). Bringing out souls from the kingdom of darkness to the kingdom of Our Lord Jesus Christ

Basis of Faith

THE BIBLE Timothy 3:15-16 We believe in both the Old and the New Testaments as the divinely inspired, infallible and authoritative Word of God, and are final in matters of faith, doctrine and practice. THE ONE TRUE GOD 2 Corinthians 13:14 1 John 5:7 Matthew 28:19 We believe in the FATHER, SON and HOLY SPIRIT as the one true God, a three In One GOD HEAD creator of the whole Universe, OMNIPOTENT, OMNIPRESENT, OMNISCIENT and RIGHTEOUS in His ways JESUS CHRIST Philippians 2:6 -11 Colossians 1:14 1 Thessalonians 4:16 Revelation 22:20 We believe in Jesus Christ as the Incarnate Son of God. We believe in His deity, His virgin Birth,His sinless nature, His atoning death and bodily resurrection, His ascension to the right hand of the Father, His continual intercession for the saints, His personal return to earth in power and glory,and His rule as the King of kings and Lord of lords. HOLY SPIRIT Acts 1:8 1 Corinthians 12:4 Ephesians 4:7-12 Galatians 5:22 We believe in the Holy Spirit and His baptism, with the signs of speaking in tongues following His baptism, His indwelling of the believer with the manifestation of the GIFTS and FRUIT OF THE SPIRIT for the edification of the body of Christ, and for the perfecting of the Saints for the work of the Kingdom. THE CHURCH Ephesians 5:23 We believe in the Church as the body of Christ and encompassing all true believers of the world;with Jesus Christ our Lord and Saviour as HEAD. MAN’S DEPRAVED NATURE Romans 6:23 Ephesians. 2:1 1 Corinthians 5:21 We accept the Universal sinfulness and guilt of all human persons since “the fall”, rendering them subject to God’s wrath and condemnation, and therefore need repentance and regeneration. THE SACRAMENT OF BAPTISM Luke 2:22-34 Mark 10:16 We believe in water Baptism by immersion as a clear testimony of a convert who has attained responsibleage of 12 years above. We do not baptize children but bring them for dedication unto the Lord. THE SACRAMENT OF THE LORD’S SUPPER Mark 16:16 Luke 22:9-20 1 Corinthian 11:23-24 Matthew 26:26-30 We believe in the breaking of bread in remembrance of the Lord’s atonement. NEXT LIFE John 5:28-29 Revelation 20:11-15 Daniel 12:12 We believe in the resurrection of both saved and unsaved; those that are saved unto the resurrection of eternal life and those that are unsaved unto the resurrection of eternal punishment. SALVATION Romans 3:20 – 25 2 Corinthian 5:21 Ephesians 2:8 – 9 We believe that Salvation is a gift of God through FAITH in the redemptive work of Jesus Christ on the CROSS and not attained by works. DIVINE HEALING Isaiah 53:4 – 5 1 Peter 2:24 Mark 16:18 3 John 2 We believe that Divine Healing is available for God’s people in the atonement that might be appropriated by FAITH and PRAYER in the name of Jesus Christ. We also believe in qualified medical help. TITHES,FIRST FRUITS & OFFERINGS Malachi 3:8-12 2 Corinthian 9:7 Luke 6:38 John 8:39 We believe in the tithing and first fruit of believers as the Bible clearly teaches us that a tenth of your income to the Lord, and giving of offerings for the propagation of the gospel and work of the ministry. The Lord by promise also blesses the giver and meets his needs

History

Kingdom Harvesters Ministries, affectionately called KHM by its members, is an iconical and a life-transforming church in the City of Takoradi, Ghana and in the United Kingdom. It was founded by Pastor Edwin Paddy Acquah and Lady Pastor Dorcas Paddy Acquah, on the 24th of December, 2007. KHM, through the past years, has grown into a multi national church with people from different nationalities attending our service. Since the inception of KHM, it is noted for its strong and uncompromising stands for righteousness, preaching the unadulterated word of God, the demonstration of power, the oppressed and possessed being delivered. It is a place where the love of God is broadly shared among members .It is a place where people are vertically connected to God and horizontally to their fellow humans.
